Privacy Policy for TeamXMusic

@SelxMusicBot

Introduction

Welcome to TeamXMusic! This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you interact with our bot, @SelxMusicBot, and use our services. By using TeamXMusic, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Data Collected:

  • Chat IDs and User IDs: We collect and store chat IDs and user IDs to enable the bot's functionality.
  • Current Play Data: We track active music sessions and users interacting with the bot.
  • Bot Statistics: Usage statistics like total users and chats are stored and accessible via the /stats command.

Data Storage:

  • All data is stored securely in our backend and MongoDB databases.
  • Data is updated regularly and stored permanently.

2. Data Usage

  • Functionality: Data ensures smooth bot operation, including processing commands and managing playback.
  • Analyzing Usage Patterns: We analyze data to improve performance and features.
  • Growth Understanding: Data helps us monitor TeamXMusic's growth for future improvements.
  • Sharing with Advertisers: General bot statistics (not personal data) may be shared with advertisers.

3. Data Protection

  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data.
  • Restricted Access: Only the owner has access to stored data.

4. User Rights

  • Data Removal Requests: Users can request data deletion via our support group Teams X Chat.
  • Group Data Removal: Admin status proof is required for group-related data removal.
  • Privacy Concerns: Contact us through the support group for any privacy concerns.

5. Minors' Data

  • Intended Use: TeamXMusic is available for all Telegram users, including minors.
  • Data Handling: We handle all user data equally, with no special provisions for minors.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

  • Policy Updates: We may update this policy periodically.
  • Notification: Updates will be announced in our updates channel Team X Update.

7. Data Controller

  • Ownership and Control: The owner exclusively controls the collected data.
  • For privacy concerns, contact the owner at Sushil Maurya or via the support group Teams X Chat.

Conclusion

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to handling your data responsibly and securely. By using TeamXMusic, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
